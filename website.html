My Senior Project Experience

My name is Brittany Taga and I was the project lead for the Project Development Team at Northern Arizona University. I majored in Mechanical Engineering and Compariative Cultural Studies with an emphasis in Asian and North African Studies and did a study abroad in Japan at Kyushu University, Fukuoka. I created this website to not only create a working website but to also reflect on my experience with the Collegiate Wind Competition (CWC) hosted by the Department of Energy.

For the 2020-2021 compeition, the teams were tasked with creating and designing a turbine, creating a project management plan for a 100MW wind farm in the western part of South Dakota, and to connect with people in the renewable energy industry along with an outreach to get younger people involved. Note: Every year, the Department of Energy changes the rules. Therefore, the final deliverables varies from year to year.

In the About, you can find more information about the competition along with specific rules for the 2020-2021 competition.

In Experience, I talk about how I went through the competition as a student and as a Project Development lead.

In Offical Project Websites, there are links to the turbine, project development, and electrical team's official school websites.
